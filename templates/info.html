*** You can enter single or multiple peptides to search for peptides. To search for more than one peptide is sufficient to make a carriage return.
*** Use the fasta format to search for peptides from the file.
*** To estimate anti-cancer peptides; Residue-Couple Model properties of the peptides were removed and then the characteristics of the extracted properties were done with the Blosum 90 matrix. In order to increase the effect of the study results, using the Partition Membership method of the feature selection methods, the size of the properties is reduced and classified by classifying algorithms. Anti-cancer peptides were obtained by using the DPC Tree algorithm, 96.51% accuracy, 96.38% sensitivity, 96.60% specificity and 0.928 mcc values were obtained.
